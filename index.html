Yoga Alliance Certification - Globally Recognized Yoga Training

RYS 200, RYS 300, RYS 500

Nirvana Yoga School is an RYS 200, RYS 300, and RYS 500 registered yoga school situated in Rishikesh, with Yoga Alliance USA-strictly considered one of the best globally respected bodies in yoga education. Our certification allows you to teach yoga with confidence anywhere in the world. Each course is designed very carefully with proper traditional knowledge, safety standards, and personal transformation. Here, certifications are a little more than just a paper; one truly becomes a yogi! Begin your journey as a certified yoga teacher with international-level credentials right here in the heart of Rishikesh, India.

Our RYS 200 curriculum is ideal for students who are new to yoga or wish to expand their knowledge of the discipline. The principles of yoga philosophy, anatomy, asana, pranayama, meditation, and teaching style are covered in this curriculum. After completing this course, you will be able to teach yoga sessions in a safe and effective manner.

Our RYS 300 course is for people who have already finished an RYS 200 course and wish to advance their knowledge and teaching skills. This curriculum delves into more advanced topics including yoga sequencing, modifications, and therapeutic applications. After completing this course, you will have a better grasp of yoga practice and the ability to teach with confidence and experience.

Our RYS 500 course is a combination of RYS 200 + RYS 300 courses. The curriculum often includes extensive study and practice on a wide range of topics from beginner to advanced level. After completing this course, you will have received the highest level of yoga teacher training possible and will be completely prepared to teach advanced yoga.
